Frequently Asked Questions

Why did you build this?

Moogle scratches a very specific itch.

Email newsletters are hard to find. Your content often sits in someone's inbox unread while people who need it don't even know you exist.

I wanted to make emails social to improve their chances of being organically discovered, gathering an audience, and going viral.

Why should use Moogle?

At its core, Moogle gives you a shareable link for every email.

You can bookmark the link, share it on WhatsApp, monitor its long term search performance

Do you offer more secure ways to share emails?

More secure ways to share emails are in the works. They will be aimed at businesses and more privacy conscious users.

Can I duplicate this?

Of course!

My source code is hosted on Github here. Just clone the repo, run http-server . (or any such webserver for static files) on the command line and you'll be good to go.

Can I contribute?

Of course!

Check out the issues page on my Github for this project and send me a PR for any issue that you can help with.

Why is the website called Moogle?

Moogle as in "Google®" but for eMails.
